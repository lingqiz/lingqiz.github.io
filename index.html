Hi, this is Lingqi, wellcome to my website!

I'm a first year PhD student at the University of Pennsylvania working at the Computational Perception and Cognition Lab, which is also part of the Computational Neuroscience Initiative.

I did my undergrad in Computer Science, at Southern University of Science and Technology, which is newly established in 2011 at Shenzhen, China. (Short story about our university)

Previously I worked at Multisensory Perception Lab at University of California, Los Angeles with Professor Ladan Shams, where I study the computational principle behind multisensory integration (Bayesian causal inference). I've also been working with Professor Jeffrey Erlich at New York University Shanghai on computational modeling of rodents behavior. I closely interact with Professor Qi Wang during my undergradaute, whose guidance had a significant influence on me doing great science.
